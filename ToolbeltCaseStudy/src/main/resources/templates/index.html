Helping Communities Build and Repair

Community Toolbelt is an application designed to assist organizations in serving the needs of the community. The primary types of projects are painting, repairs, and building. This site will help in estimating materials and tools needed for builng ramps, porches, and interior painting.

The intent of this project is to help serve both those who need the assistance and those community teams who are using their tools to make the world a better place.

We must be the change we want to see in this world.
